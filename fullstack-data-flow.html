The Restaurant Analogy

Every web app works like a restaurant. Three players, one flow.

🍽️
Frontend
The Menu & Table
What the customer sees. They read the menu, place an order, and wait for food. They never go to the kitchen.
👨‍🍳
Backend
The Waiter + Kitchen
The waiter (API) takes the order, checks if the customer is allowed to order alcohol (Authorization), and passes it to the kitchen (Logic).
🏪
Database
The Storage Room
All ingredients (data) are stored here. The kitchen pulls what it needs. Without it, you'd have to put all the food on the table upfront (Hardcoded).
